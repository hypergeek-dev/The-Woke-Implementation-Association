What we Do

The project aims to create a contiguous forest area over a vast area in the US that will sequester carbon emissions and provide habitat for wildlife. It is led by a coalition of groups.

We respect wild animals as individuals with their own needs and preferences, rather than seeing them as mere parts of ecosystems. Deforestation is a critical issue that affects our world in many ways. Trees and forests are essential for the health and stability of our planet. They play an important role in mitigating climate change, regulating the water cycle, maintaining biodiversity and providing resources for people and wildlife.
