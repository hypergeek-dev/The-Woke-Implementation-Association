Educational Links

The following is a a comprehensive list of organisations we support and work with. Please also read about their work and learn more about preserving the planet.

Traffic

Traffic is an organisation that work to ensure that the trade of wild plats and animals is not a threat to the conservation of nature.

Wildlife Conservation Trust

This organisation's focus is to safeguard life-giving ecosystems in a holistic, sustainable manner. They place equal emphasis on both forest and wildlife conservation.

Wildlife Heritage Areas

This organisation allows local people to recognise their natural heritage at sites the meet the highest standards of sustainable tourism.

PAWS

PAWS is an organisation dedicated to the preservation of wildlife. They operate their own animal hospital and provide a hotline to assist others in protecting wildlife.
